बिमल कुमार बसु, एक विद्युत अभियन्ता, कृत्रिम बुद्धि अनुसंधानकर्ता, शिक्षक और प्रोफेसर हैं। सम्प्रति वे नॉक्सविल्ली के तेनीसी विश्वविद्यालय में विद्युत इंजीनियरी और संगणक विज्ञान के प्रोफेसर हैं। 
Bose is the sole author/editor of seven books in power electronics some of which have been translated into several foreign languages, and some of which are widely used as texts throughout the world. His book "Power Electronics and AC Drives" is the first English language text book in power electronics and motor drives area.
