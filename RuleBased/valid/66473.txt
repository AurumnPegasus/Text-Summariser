In मेजर लीग बेसबॉल, the 500 होम रन क्लब is an informal term applied to the group of players who have hit 500 or more career home runs. The first member of the 500 home run club was Babe Ruth in 1929.
Every player with at least 500 homers who is eligible for the Major League Baseball Hall of Fame has been inducted except for Mark McGwire.
